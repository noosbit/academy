George De'Ath
Co-Founder & CCO

George puts the 'born' in Born & Bred. Back in 2017, the agency was a wild dream of his. Since then, the company has grown wild with ideas, people, and bad British accents aimed at his expense.

Culture constantly occupies George’s mind, amongst a million other things. But for him, a company creates meaningful work when people feel purpose in what they do. If the team thrives, so will our output.

Prior to Born & Bred, George worked at agencies in London, Oxford, and San Francisco. There he gained extensive experience amongst multiple industries—including global brands like Samsung, Diageo, and L'Oréal. Working on these legacy brands helped George discover his vision: to bring soul and energy back into design.

“I never thought it would grow to the team we have today. Watching our family develop, seeing new people start, and adding new diverse perspectives—it's pretty special to me.”

 

  • george@bornandbredbrand.com
Kyle Merwin
Co-Founder & Strategy Director

Leading strategy and new business, Kyle Merwin makes up 50% of the founding team at Born & Bred. Kyle first linked up with his future partner, George De'Ath, in 2017 as B&B's first Strategy & Growth Consultant. After a few successful collaborations together, George asked him to be the Chewie to his Han Solo for this Millenium Falcon of a company he was building. Kyle accepted with zero hesitation; and the two have continued to create and grow brands at hyperspeed ever since.  

Prior to B&B, Kyle was a ground-floor employee (#18) at the cannabis-tech startup Eaze.  At the intersection of growth and brand, Kyle created and managed programs that grew Eaze to over 1 million deliveries without the use of paid digital channels. A few of these programs include: college brand ambassadors, content (SEO, Video, Social), white-label brand creation, and the Eaze Insights Report series—which was the company's #1 driver of awareness and  covered by the New Yorker, New York Times, Forbes, AdAge, and dozens more.

Fun stuff: Kyle was born in La Paz, Bolivia but grew up in Marin County. He is a proud graduate of UC Berkeley (Go Bears!) and is a huge Giants & Warriors fan (sorry, not a football guy). Kyle loves Hip-Hop, beach vacations, Hazy IPAs, Popeye's Chicken, and making people laugh.

  • kyle@bornandbredbrand.com

Ryan Spiers
Associate Creative Director

You want words? This ACD's got ‘em: Xylophone, Eureka, Platypus. Consider those freebies. But words are only a small, vital part of Ryan’s vision to create holistic brands that stand out at every level. Because this writer doesn’t just have an eye for design—he has two.

Ryan was actually born and bred in San Francisco. Something you can hear him both boast and complain about. After graduating from UC Berkeley, he was on track to be a lawyer. Until he said, “I Object!”—pun obnoxiously intended—and made the career jump into branding. Whether it’s for Nike, Microsoft, or any business, for him, seeing something that came from his brain out in the wild never gets old. That’s what he wants to help create here—a team that produces inspiring, unique, daring work that we can point to proudly in public. 

 

  • ryan@bornandbredbrand.com

Bill Braznell
Production Director

Bill is interested in opportunities where he can offer both technical dexterity and innovative thought—always with the goal of demonstrating how sophisticated production is a critical part of design. As a production strategist, production designer, production manager, and mechanical artist, Bill has an uncommon knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite and an eagerness to share it.

Bill has over 20 years of experience creating press-ready mechanicals for complex packaging products—and along the way has acquired the foresight required to prevent mishap. Bill transforms conceptual artwork into structurally sound mechanicals and extension systems by way of thoughtful strategy and technical acuity. Constantly swiveling between 4000% magnification and the big picture, Bill creates precisely constructed files and the artful processes in which they flow.

Anastasia Itkina
Senior Designer

Known as the ‘vet’ of Born & Bred, Anastasia became B&B’s first employee as a senior graphic designer in 2018. The Russian-born designer came to America for three main reasons: her passion for nature, to work with the Latin alphabet, and, of course, a love story.

Besides just being a graphic designer, Anastasia serves as a printmaker, an illustrator, a copywriter, a production designer, and sometimes even a scientist. The reason for having this diverse skill set, as Anastasia explains it, is her secret ability to quickly pick up new techniques and passion for learning.

Working at an agency has allowed Anastasia to look inside all possible industries and learn about other peoples’ jobs through her projects. In her tenure here, she has already worked with a technological coffee brand, a florist boutique, and learned how bioengineering works for a hangover cure. If you’re interested in getting in touch with her, we recommend searching the local nurseries on weekends. She’ll be there talking to the plants.

  • anastasia@bornandbredbrand.com
Mariel Zayas-Bazan
Senior Copywriter

Eloquently crass, Mariel is hell-bent on shifting paradigms with sharp words and slick design. She once convinced a jehovah’s witness to listen to Black Sabbath as a means to connect with non-believers. 

A sweetheart from the South, she bar-tended her way through her BFA, fascinated with the human condition on the rocks. After graduating from Savannah College of Art & Design she translated her interest to long form journalism, freelancing for Inked Magazine, and Vice Media, among other hip publications. 

Adapting her love of the craft to more shootable executions, Mariel joined the marketing world in Miami, where her bilingual upbringing appealed to brands like Hertz, KFC, and anyone looking for solid messaging with cultural cues. She’s versatile. Pitching health & wellness by day and drumming in a death doom band by night, contradictions excite her and make the whole 9-5 thing worthwhile.

An Huang
Designer

An Huang is a Taiwanese designer and a boba devourer. With a background in Sociology, An struggled to put the theories she learned into practice. So she took a quick detour to the US to study design. Because for her, design is a practical, hands-on way to helping the world become a better place.  

That self-described “naive” idea still inspires An today. Her design works to unveil ideas and notions that are worth spreading and noticing. And even if it doesn’t , An knows prettier things make people happier in their everyday life. 

Outside of design, An enjoys musicals (current top three: Hamilton, Wicked, and Miss Saigon), karaoke with friends, and playing the piano on a cozy weekend morning.

Careers

Help us make this thing called work even better.

Copywriter

Hello brilliance-typing, headline-sniping, copy-writing individual.

Born & Bred is looking for a Copywriter to join our team. Really we want someone who loves to create and is pretty damn good at doing so. Born & Bred is a rapidly growing agency where you’ll find yourself with significant creative freedom and ownership. This is your moment for the taking. If you want to see the impact of your work, you’ve found the perfect role.


And while it’s important to bring in talented people, it’s also important to bring in people people. That means someone who will add to our office culture and make this thing called work even better.
Apply here
  • At least 3 years of agency experience.
  • Words get you excited. Maybe a little too much.
  • Be in the know. You’re up to date on what’s popular, trendy, or the current zeitgeist.
  • Willingness to fight for ideas worth fighting for.
  • Know how to write for various media formats. Not just formatting, but also in tone and voice.
  • Ability to handle multiple creative projects simultaneously.
  • College degree isn’t necessary, but might as well put it down if you got one.
  • A resume and a portfolio of your greatest hits.

Senior Designer

Hello, searcher.

Born & Bred is looking for a Senior Designer to join our team. Really we want someone who loves to create and is pretty damn good at doing so.
